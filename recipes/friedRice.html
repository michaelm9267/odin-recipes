Fried Rice

Cook Time:

15 minutes

Ingredients

  • ⅔ cup chopped baby carrots
  • ½ cup frozen green peas
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced, or to taste (Optional)
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 cups leftover cooked and chilled white rice
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce, or more to taste
  • 2 teaspoons sesame oil, or to taste

Preparation

  1. Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the chopped baby carrots and cook for 3-4 minutes until slightly softened.
  3. Add the frozen green peas and cook for an additional 2 minutes.
  4. Push the vegetables to one side of the skillet, and pour the remaining 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil into the empty side.
  5. Add the minced garlic and cook until fragrant, about 30 seconds.
  6. Crack the eggs into the skillet and scramble until fully cooked, then mix with the vegetables.
  7. Add the chilled rice to the skillet and break up any clumps using a spatula.
  8. Stir in the soy sauce and sesame oil, ensuring the rice is evenly coated.
  9. Cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the rice is heated through and slightly crispy.
  10. Taste and adjust seasoning with additional soy sauce or sesame oil if needed.
  11. Serve immediately and enjoy!
