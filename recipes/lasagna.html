World's Best Lasagna

Cook Time:

1 hour 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • Meat: This super meaty lasagna has sweet Italian sausage and lean ground beef.
  • Onion and Garlic: An onion and two cloves of garlic are cooked with the meat to add tons of flavor.
  • Tomato Products: You'll need a can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce, and two cans of tomato paste.
  • Sugar: Two tablespoons of white sugar add subtle sweetness and enhance the flavor of the sauce.
  • Spices and Seasonings: This lasagna recipe is flavored with fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper.
  • Lasagna Noodles: Use store-bought or homemade lasagna noodles.
  • Cheeses: Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese make this lasagna extra decadent.
  • Egg: An egg helps bind the ricotta so it doesn't ooze out of the lasagna when you cut into it.

Preparation

  1. In a large skillet, cook the sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until well browned. Drain excess fat.
  2. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, and sugar. Add basil, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, salt, and black pepper to taste. Simmer for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, and parsley. Mix well and set aside.
  4. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  5. Spread 1½ cups of the meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  6. Arrange a layer of lasagna noodles over the meat sauce. Spread ⅓ of the ricotta mixture on top. Sprinkle with mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Repeat layers, ending with a final layer of meat sauce and cheeses on top.
  7. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil (to prevent sticking, either spray the foil with cooking spray or ensure it doesn’t touch the cheese).
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake an additional 25 minutes, or until the lasagna is bubbling and slightly golden on top.
  9. Let the lasagna cool for 15 minutes before serving.
